Privacy Policy

SSolstice respects your privacy. We do not collect personally identifiable information unless it is necessary for app functionality or required by platform services such as Google Play.

Some applications may use anonymized analytics or crash reporting tools to help us understand usage patterns and improve app stability. No personal data is sold or shared with third parties for advertising.

If you have any questions regarding privacy, you can contact us through the support email listed on Google Play.
