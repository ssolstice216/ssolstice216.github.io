Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

S²olstice respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile applications and related services ("Services").

By using our Services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

We do not require you to provide personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address in order to use our applications.

b. User Content

Our applications may allow you to create or store photos, videos, notes, or other content on your device ("User Content").

User Content remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to share, export, or back up this content using third-party services.

S²olstice does not access, upload, or store your personal photos, videos, or notes on our servers.

c. Automatically Collected Information

We may collect limited, non-personal information automatically, including:

  • Application version
  • Device model and operating system
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics

This information is collected solely to improve app stability, performance, and reliability.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected is used only for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining core app functionality
  • Improving performance and user experience
  • Identifying and fixing bugs or crashes
  • Complying with legal obligations

We do not use your information for advertising, tracking, or profiling purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Our applications may integrate third-party services provided by Google or other providers, including but not limited to:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing
  • Firebase (for crash reporting or analytics, if enabled)

These third-party services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies. S²olstice does not control and is not responsible for the data practices of third-party services.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Information may be disclosed only if required by law or in response to valid legal requests by public authorities.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, no method of transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your information, including the right to request access, correction, or deletion where applicable.

You may contact us at any time regarding privacy-related questions or concerns.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
