Creative apps, crafted with intention

SSolstice is an independent mobile app studio focused on building photography and utility applications that value simplicity, performance, and thoughtful design.

Our apps are inspired by classic tools such as film cameras, mechanical dials, and paper notebooks. We design software that feels calm, deliberate, and respectful of the user’s attention.

Privacy-first

We prioritize user privacy and avoid unnecessary data collection. Our apps are designed to work locally whenever possible.

Designed for creators

From photographers to writers, our tools focus on control, clarity, and creative flow rather than automation overload.

Minimal & reliable

We believe fewer features — done well — create better products. Stability and performance are always our priorities.
