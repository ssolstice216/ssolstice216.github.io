About SSolstice

SSolstice is an independent mobile development studio focused on creating high-quality creative and photography applications for Android.

Our products are designed with a strong emphasis on visual clarity, performance, and a deep respect for classic photography tools. From vintage film simulations to professional manual camera controls, we aim to deliver apps that feel both modern and timeless.

SSolstice applications are built for photographers, creators, and users who value simplicity, reliability, and thoughtful design. We continuously improve our apps based on user feedback and evolving mobile technologies.

Thank you for supporting independent development.

Help & Support

Welcome to SSolstice support. Below are answers to common questions and guidance for using our applications.

Getting Started

Most SSolstice apps are designed to work out of the box. Simply grant the required permissions (such as camera or storage) when prompted, and you can begin using the core features immediately.

Camera & Permissions

If a feature is not working as expected, please check that all required permissions are enabled in your device settings. Some camera features may not be supported on all devices due to hardware limitations.

Film Rolls & Storage

Photos and videos are saved locally on your device unless otherwise stated. Uninstalling the app may remove locally stored content, so please back up important files before deleting the app.

Performance Issues

If you experience crashes or performance problems, ensure that your app is updated to the latest version. Restarting the app or device may also help resolve temporary issues.
