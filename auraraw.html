Intro

AuraRAW is a rich and powerful camera app filled with features, modes and more. It may seem overwhelming at first, but it is actually easy to master! This guide will teach you how to get the most out of AuraRAW, and share tips along the way. It assumes no prior photography or filmmaking experience, and can be read by users of all skill levels.

Viewfinder

The viewfinder is where the camera feed is shown. Here you can preview your scene, select focus and exposure points, and more. In addition, any changes to zoom or exposure compensation values will show at the top of the viewfinder.

Focus and Exposure

To set the focus and exposure point, tap anywhere on the viewfinder. To reposition the point, drag the box anywhere you like. To reset, tap the box.

Zoom

To zoom, simply drag one finger up or down on the viewfinder.

Spot Metering

The default exposure metering is center-weighted. To set it to spot metering, tap the AE button twice, once for AE-L, and again for AE spot metering.

Tip: Tap the zoom or exposure compensation labels to reset their value.

Top & Bottom Bars

Top Bar

  • Preview Image: Shows a preview of the most recently captured photo or video.
  • Grid Overlay: Toggles between different grid options (Rule of Thirds, Golden Ratio).
  • Aspect Ratio: Select between 4:3, 16:9, 1:1 and custom aspect ratios.
  • Settings: Opens the deep settings panel for histogram, themes and more.

Bottom Bar

Contains access to many of ProShot's camera settings including Flash, White Balance, ISO, Shutter Speed, and Focus.

Tip: AuraRAW's menu system is designed to keep related items grouped together.

Camera Dial

The Camera Dial is modeled after a traditional DSLR mode dial and was the inspiration for AuraRAW's UI.

Auto

Use this mode to capture great results in any situation, without needing to configure anything.

Manual

Full control over ISO, shutter speed, and focus. The ultimate way to craft your vision.

Custom

C1 and C2 allow you to save and recall your favorite settings instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is V9.0? +
V9.0 is a major update rolling out in stages. Please check the Google Play Store for the latest availability in your region.
Can I change the aperture? +
On mobile devices, aperture is usually fixed by the hardware. AuraRAW allows software emulation for specific depth-of-field effects on supported devices.
My device has multiple cameras, why can't AuraRAW use them? +
We work hard to support all lenses. Some manufacturers restrict third-party access to auxiliary lenses. Check the "Camera Groups" section in Technical Notes for more info.
