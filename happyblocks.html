Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Happy Blocks game collects and uses information when you play it.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. However, the game may collect non-personal data:

  • Gameplay events (score, session time)
  • Device information (model, OS)
  • Crash logs

2. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following services:

  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Firebase Remote Config
  • (Optional) Ad networks such as AdMob/AppLovin

3. Cookies / Tracking

No cookies are used. Analytics SDKs may process anonymized data.
