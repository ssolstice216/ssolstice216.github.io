Common Questions

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Ensure you are logged into the same Google account used for the purchase, then tap "Restore" in the app settings.

Why isn't RAW capture working?

RAW (DNG) support depends on your device's hardware and manufacturer support for the Camera2 API.

Can I export data from Fieldly?

Yes, Fieldly supports exporting site documentation as PDF, CSV, and KML files.
