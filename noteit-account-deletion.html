Account & Data Deletion

NoteIt — S²olstice · Updated: December 2025

This page explains how users of NoteIt (developed by S²olstice) can request permanent deletion of their account and associated personal data. It also describes what data will be removed, what may be retained for legal reasons, and how long we will keep retained records.

How to request deletion

You can request deletion using one of the following methods. Please include the requested identifiers so we can locate and verify your account quickly.

Email (recommended)

Send an email to our data protection team:

Request account deletion via email

In your message, please include:

  • The email address registered with your NoteIt account (if any)
  • Your display name (if applicable)
  • A short confirmation sentence such as: "I request permanent deletion of my NoteIt account and all associated data."

We will acknowledge your request within 3 business days and aim to complete deletion within 7 business days after identity verification.

In-App request (if available)

If you are signed in, go to Settings → Account → Delete account and follow the on-screen steps. Deletion requests submitted inside the app will follow the same verification and timelines as email requests.

What we delete

When a deletion request is completed, we permanently remove the following from our production systems and backups according to the timelines below:

Data category Removed? Notes / retention
Account profile (email, display name) Yes Deleted immediately from active systems
User content (notes, drawings, checklists) Yes Permanently deleted; may remain in backups for up to 30 days
Uploaded media (images, attachments) Yes Deleted from storage; may persist in backups for up to 30 days
Subscription / purchase records Partially Transactional records retained for financial reconciliation and legal compliance (up to 7 years as required)
Server logs & diagnostics No (limited retention) Kept for security and fraud prevention; retained up to 30 days unless longer retention required by law

What we may keep

In certain cases we may retain minimal records for legal, regulatory or fraud prevention purposes (for example, to comply with tax or accounting obligations). Retained data is minimized and access is strictly controlled.

Verification & security

To protect your account from unauthorized deletion, we will verify ownership before processing a deletion request. Verification may include:

  • Confirming the registered email address
  • Asking for recent transaction details (if you have a paid subscription)
  • Requesting additional identity information in exceptional cases

Local device data

Deleting your online account does not automatically remove any notes or files that remain stored locally on your device. To remove local data you can:

  • Open the app and manually delete notes/media.
  • Uninstall the app (note: some devices keep local app data until manually cleared).

Third-party services

If you used cloud sync (e.g., Firebase, Google Drive) or linked third-party services, data stored by those services will be removed according to their retention and deletion policies. We will coordinate deletion requests involving third-party providers where possible.

Appeals & questions

If you have questions about deletion or believe your data was not removed properly, contact our support team at ssolstice216@gmail.com. We will investigate and respond promptly.
