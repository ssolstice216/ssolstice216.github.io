Account Deletion

Last updated: December 2025

This page explains how users of noteIt can request permanent deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to request deletion

1. Email Request (Recommended)

Send an email to our team at:

mail Request account deletion

Please include your registered email address and a short confirmation of your request.

2. In-App Request

Go to Settings → Account → Delete account inside the app and follow the on-screen steps.

What we delete

Data category Removed? Retention
Account profile Yes Immediately
User notes & content Yes Permanently (up to 30 days in backups)
Uploaded media Yes Deleted from active storage
Purchase records Partially Retained for legal/tax compliance

Verification & Timelines

We will acknowledge your request within 3 business days and aim to complete the deletion within 7 business days after verifying your identity.

Contact

If you have questions about deletion or believe your data was not removed properly, contact our support team at ssolstice216@gmail.com.
