About S²olstice

S²olstice is an independent software studio dedicated to creating high-quality mobile applications with a strong focus on design, usability, and long-term reliability.

We believe that good software should respect the user’s attention. Instead of adding features for the sake of complexity, we focus on refining essential tools and making them feel natural and dependable.

Many of our design inspirations come from analog tools — film cameras, mechanical dials, and paper notebooks. These influences guide the way we design interfaces that feel deliberate and human.
